# 2. Design Phase 
Main Outputs: SRS, High-Level Design (HLD), and Low-Level Design (LLD). 
Tools: Figma (UI/UX), Lucidchart (Architecture), ER Diagrams (Database). 
System vs. UI/UX: System focuses on the backbone (APIs, DB); UI/UX focuses on the skin and feel. 
Failure Example: Knight Capital Group (2012) - $440 million loss due to design flaw.
